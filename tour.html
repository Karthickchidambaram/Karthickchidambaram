Meenakshi Amman Temple

Famous temple dedicated to Sundareswarar and Goddess Meenakshi — a masterpiece of Dravidian architecture and a living cultural centre.

Thirumalai Nayak Palace

A 17th-century royal palace with grand pillars, stucco work and vast courtyards — a window into Madurai's regal past.

Gandhi Memorial Museum

Located in Tamukkam Palace, this museum houses important relics and documents related to Mahatma Gandhi and India's freedom movement.
